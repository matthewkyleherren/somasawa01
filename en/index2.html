Infinite Matter — Turning plastic waste into art

Can art restore some optimism to the environmental crisis? From May 05 onward, TUX Karma invites you to discover how a small group of motivated people have been able to breathe new conceptual life into plastic waste. Welcome to the exhibition where ecology and art come together to deliver a great message of hope!
